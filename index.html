About

I am a first-year master's student at ECNU, working at the intersection of cognition and AI.

One direction of my research is how to analyze and evaluate AI cognition. The other direction is how to use AI to diagnose and support human cognition in learning and decision-making contexts.

I am actively seeking PhD opportunities in HCI, especially programs valuing research on human-AI collaboration and responsible AI.
